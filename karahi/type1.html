Beef Karahi

Beef Karahi is a traditional, flavorful South Asian dish made with tender pieces of beef cooked in a spicy, aromatic curry in a karahi (a deep, circular pan similar to a wok). This dish is especially popular in Pakistan and India, where it is often prepared for special occasions or family meals. It is known for its rich, bold flavors and the way the beef absorbs the spices, creating a delicious and hearty curry.
