Mutton Nihari

Mutton nihari is a slow-cooked, flavorful stew made with mutton, bone marrow, and a variety of spices

Ingredients:

Mutton, bone marrow, long pepper (pippali), and other spices

Preparation

The meat is cooked for a long time in a large pot over low heat, until it's tender and the gravy is thickened

Serving

Often eaten with naan in Pakistan and Bangladesh
