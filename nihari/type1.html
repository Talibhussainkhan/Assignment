Chicken Nihari

Chicken nihari is a slow-cooked Pakistani dish of chicken in a rich, spiced gravy:

Ingredients

Chicken, long pepper (pippali), whole wheat flour, garam masala, and other spices

Preparation

The meat is cooked for several hours in a large pot called a shab degh, and the gravy is thickened with flour and spices

Serving

Chicken nihari is often served with naan or roti.
