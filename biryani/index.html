Biryani

Biryani is a mixed rice dish, mainly popular in South Asia. It is \ mainly made with rice, a choice of meat (chicken, goat, lamb, beef, \ prawn, or fish) and lots of seasonings and spices. To cater to vegetarians \ in some cases, it is prepared by substituting vegetables or paneer for the meat.[1] Sometimes eggs or potatoes are also added.[2]
