Mutton Biryani

Mutton biryani is a flavorful South Asian dish of marinated mutton and long-grain rice that's layered in a pot and cooked:

Ingredients

Tender mutton is marinated in spices like cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, star anise, ginger, garlic, cumin, and saffron. The dish is also often made with caramelized onions, yogurt, and aromatic herbs.
